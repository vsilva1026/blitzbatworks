About

Built in the Backyard.

Blitz Bat Works started the way every great backyard idea does — with a beat-up wiffle bat, a roll of tape, and a summer afternoon.

What began as customizing bats for pickup games turned into something more: bats built to order, each one wrapped and finished by hand. No two are the same. Every bat gets its own color scheme, its own grip, its own personality.

Whether you're squaring up for a home run derby, a family cookout tournament, or you just want a bat that actually looks like yours — we've got you covered.

What Makes Us Different

Every bat is hand-taped from start to finish. You pick the colors, patterns, and grip style — we build it. No mass production, no cookie-cutter designs.
