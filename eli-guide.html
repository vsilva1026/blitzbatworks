Step 1

FIRST TIME ON A NEW DEVICE

  1. Go to blitzbatworks.com/playground.html
  2. Click the ⚙ Settings button in the left panel
  3. Type your password (ewilder26) and check "Remember on this browser"
  4. Click Save — you're set up forever on this device
Step 2

EDIT YOUR SITE

  1. Open the playground URL
  2. Change anything on the left — the preview on the right updates instantly
  3. Nothing is live yet — this is just for you to play
  4. When you like it, click the red PUBLISH button
  5. Wait for "Published! 🎉" — your real site updates in ~30 seconds
Cheat Sheet

WHAT EACH BUTTON DOES

PUBLISH
Makes your changes live at blitzbatworks.com
SAVE DRAFT
Saves work in progress (doesn't go live)
RESET
Throw away changes, load the live version
⚙ SETTINGS
Enter your password once per device
EXPORT
Download a backup file of everything
DESKTOP / MOBILE
Toggle preview to see phone view
Pro Tips

MAKE IT YOURS

  • Try different Color Themes — 16 options, whole vibe changes instantly
  • Drag section names in Section Order to rearrange your page
  • Uncheck any section to hide it entirely
  • Use Compact / Normal / Spacious to control how big each section is
  • Add photos in Gallery to show off your best builds
  • Every bat style can have its own photo — upload one for each
  • The Featured Bat section is great for a "Bat of the Week" spotlight
